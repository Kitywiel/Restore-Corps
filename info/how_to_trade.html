How to trade

Trading always carries risks, and it's easy to get scammed.
But how can you minimize your chances of being scammed?
Let's find out.

How to Know You Can Trust Someone

To know if someone can be trusted:
Ask other people what they think.
Talk to a few people in a group or community.
Hearing different opinions helps you decide safely.

Your First Meetup

To begin, pick a location where you're going to meet up.
Ideally, this should be more than 5K away from your base ‚Äî the farther, the better.
Set up a stasis chamber or a bed for the person who will be coming.
Make sure the items you want to trade are already there.

In the meetup

Make sure that when he tells you to pull, you activate the stasis chamber right away.
This is a very important part of the meetup.
If you wait too long or miss the timing, it could mess up the whole plan.
This is because they only have a small window to do this ‚Äî they're usually very busy.
Stay ready, listen carefully, and act fast when you get the signal.

After the meetup

Make sure you move your items quickly.
There is a common scam where the other person goes back to take the items you left behind.
